Autonomous Driving & ADAS

Real-world urban driving data for perception, planning, and edge-case discovery.

Why Existing Data Fails in Real Urban Driving

  • Simulation data breaks down in dense, unstructured traffic
  • Short video clips lose temporal continuity and motion context
  • Heavy anonymization removes gaze, intent, and micro-behaviors
  • Edge cases occur in transitions, not isolated frames

How This Dataset Is Collected

  • Continuous real-world driving footage (5–15 minutes per run)
  • Street-level perspective from motorcycles, cars, and urban mobility
  • Segment-level indexing (1-minute units) while preserving full continuity
  • Original and anonymized versions available depending on use case
  • Environment, density, and motion metadata attached

Typical Applications

  • Multi-object tracking in dense traffic
  • Planning and prediction in non-lane-based roads
  • Failure analysis of perception models
  • Long-tail scenario discovery in emerging markets
