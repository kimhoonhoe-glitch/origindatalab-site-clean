Who evaluates this data?

Evaluation teams often include perception engineers, robotics teams, autonomous systems researchers, and other AI teams working on complex real-world environments, urban mobility, and edge-case validation.

Why no client logos?

Many evaluation projects are conducted under NDA or early-stage research agreements, so customer identities are not publicly disclosed. Instead, we publish transparent schemas, QC summaries, and sample structures so teams can evaluate the data before purchase.

We do not publish inflated dataset volumes. Coverage is continuously expanding — we share a current snapshot on request.
