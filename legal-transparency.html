Buyer Approval FAQ

A practical review block for procurement, legal, and compliance teams before purchase approval.

Is the data collected on public roads?

Yes. The dataset is collected in publicly accessible road environments as part of real-world urban traffic capture.

Are faces and license plates blurred by default?

Blurring or anonymization can be applied based on buyer requirements, jurisdictional needs, and approved delivery scope.

Can procurement teams review licensing terms before purchase?

Yes. Public summary information is available in advance, and more detailed licensing materials can be shared during qualification or under NDA where needed.

Is a DPA available on request?

Yes. Data Processing Agreement support can be discussed when procurement or legal review requires it.

Can provenance documentation be shared under NDA?

Yes. More detailed provenance, handling, and compliance-related materials can be provided during formal review under NDA.

Data Provenance FAQ

Key operational and governance details for security review, provenance validation, and engineering confidence.

How is the data collected?

Real-world mobile capture is performed through structured field collection workflows designed for traffic, environment, and scenario diversity.

What anonymization standards are applied?

Sensitive elements such as faces, license plates, and other identifying details can be masked, filtered, or handled according to buyer and compliance requirements.

Is raw data preserved?

Yes. Raw continuous footage may be preserved for traceability, auditability, and controlled downstream processing, while delivery scope remains segment-based.

How are minors or sensitive scenes handled?

Sensitive or review-flagged content can be filtered, restricted, or excluded based on policy, legal requirements, and delivery scope.

Is cross-border transfer involved?

Data handling and storage may involve controlled cross-border workflows depending on buyer requirements, hosting structure, and compliance constraints.

What infrastructure is used?

Cloud-based storage, controlled access, and traceable handling processes are used to support delivery integrity, audit readiness, and operational security.
