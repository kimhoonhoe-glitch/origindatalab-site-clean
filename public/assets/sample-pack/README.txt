Origindata Lab – Sample Pack

This sample pack is designed to help you quickly evaluate real-world dataset structure, quality, and integration fit for AI training and perception systems.

---

What’s included

- Sample video clips (short segments from real urban environments)
- Metadata preview (JSON format)
- Schema preview (field structure reference)
- QC summary (basic validation signals)
- Technical Summary (PDF)
- License Summary (PDF)

---

How to use this sample pack

This pack is intended for quick internal review and validation.

You can use it to:

- Assess scene complexity and real-world variability
- Review clip format and segment structure
- Understand metadata fields and organization
- Evaluate compatibility with your pipeline

---

About the data

All clips are collected from real urban environments with high variability, including:

- Mixed traffic (cars, motorcycles, pedestrians)
- Unstructured road behavior
- High-density interactions
- Real-world noise and ambiguity

This data is not simulated or filtered.
